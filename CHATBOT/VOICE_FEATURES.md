## üîä Voice Output (Text-to-Speech)

The chatbot automatically speaks all responses aloud, making it perfect for elderly users and those who prefer listening.

### How It Works

1. When you receive a response from the chatbot
2. The response is **automatically spoken aloud**
3. You'll hear the reply in the language you selected
4. You can also read it in the chat window

### Voice Toggle

- **Speaker icon** (unmuted) in the header = Voice is ON
- **Muted icon** in the header = Voice is OFF

Click the icon to toggle voice on/off at any time.

### Language Support

- **English**: Uses native English voices
- **Malayalam**: Uses available Malayalam or Hindi voices (closest match)

### Voice Settings

- **Speed**: Slightly slower (0.85x) for clarity
- **Volume**: Full volume
- **Pitch**: Natural pitch

### Browser Support

- ‚úÖ **All modern browsers** support Text-to-Speech
- Voices may vary by browser and operating system

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Microphone Not Working

1. **Check browser permissions**: Allow microphone access
2. **Check browser support**: Chrome/Edge/Safari recommended
3. **Check microphone**: Ensure your microphone is working
4. **Try refreshing**: Reload the page and try again

### Voice Not Speaking

1. **Check mute button**: Ensure voice is not muted (speaker icon should be visible)
2. **Check volume**: Ensure your device volume is up
3. **Check browser**: Try a different browser if issues persist
4. **Check language**: Ensure you've selected a language first

### Voice Language Wrong

1. The voice uses the language you selected when opening the chat
2. Ensure you selected the correct language (English or Malayalam)
3. Browser voices may vary - the system uses the best available voice for your selected language

## üí° Usage Tips

### For Voice Input:
